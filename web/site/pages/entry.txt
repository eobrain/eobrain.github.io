Entry-Level Research Analyst
============================

Mission Analytics Group, Inc. is a non-advocacy government consulting firm based in San Francisco, conducting program evaluation, analysis and technical assistance in health and human services.  We seek a smart, highly motivated, highly adaptable employee to join our small group. 

This position will focus in the areas of developmental disabilities, long-term care, HIV/AIDS or family services.  The employee will work with an established team of researchers and analysts.  Example responsibilities include statistical programming and data analysis, web-based research, literature review, proposal writing, and development of tables and reports.       

Required Qualifications: 
-----------------------

* Bachelor’s degree or substantial coursework in economics, statistics, public policy, biostatistics, public health, or related subject
* Excellent written and verbal English communication skills
* Expertise in Excel, PowerPoint, Word
* Interest in statistical programming
* Prior work experience

Desirable Qualifications:
------------------------

* Familiarity with one or more of the following policy areas: social services for children and youth, developmental disabilities, HIV/AIDS, or long-term care
* Coursework or work experience in statistical programming using SAS, Stata, R or SPSS
* Skills in other areas such as website design (especially Drupal), graphic design, copy-editing, or geographic information systems (GIS) are highly desirable.

This is a full-time position with benefits including medical, dental and vision insurance and company 401k contributions.  A part-time schedule is possible for the right candidate.

To apply, send cover letter and resume to jobs[at]Mission-AG.com with the position name “Analyst 2012” in the subject line.  Resumes without cover letters will not be considered. Mission Analytics Group runs pre-hire background checks, including employment verification and searches of criminal and civil records. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation or national origin.   Due to the large volume of applications, we will only contact individuals who may be considered for additional screening or possible interview.
